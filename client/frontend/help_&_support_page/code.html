Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions our users ask. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

View all FAQs arrow_forward
How do I cancel a booking? expand_more
To cancel a booking, go to your "Bookings" page, select the active booking you wish to cancel, and click "Cancel Booking". Please note that cancellation fees may apply if canceled within 24 hours of the scheduled time.
How do refunds work? expand_more
Refunds are processed to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take 5-10 business days for the funds to appear in your account after we've processed the refund.
Is my payment information secure? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and partner with certified payment processors to ensure your data is always safe and secure. We do not store your full card details on our servers.
How do I become a Helper? expand_more
Click on the "Become a Helper" link in the top navigation bar. You'll need to fill out an application, provide identification, and pass a background check before you can start accepting jobs.

Still need help?

We understand that some issues need a human touch. Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries.
