Introduction

Helpers is more than just a marketplace; it's a community built on trust. Millions of connections are made on our platform every year, and we strive to make each one a positive experience. This document outlines the behaviors we expect from both Helpers and Users to maintain a safe, fair, and welcoming environment for all.

favorite

Respect & Inclusion

At Helpers, we have zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or hate speech. We expect all interactions to be professional and courteous. Treat everyone with respect regardless of their race, religion, national origin, ethnicity, disability, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, or age.

  • Respect Boundaries: Keep communication professional and related to the service being provided.
  • No Harassment: Unwanted contact, aggressive language, or intimidation tactics are strictly prohibited.
  • Inclusion: Refusing service or declining a request based on a protected characteristic is a violation of our policy.

Safety & Reliability

Safety is paramount. Whether you are welcoming a Helper into your home or entering a client's workspace, everyone should feel secure. Reliability builds the trust that our platform depends on.

For Helpers

Arrive on time, bring the necessary tools, and perform the work to the quality standard expected. Ensure you pass all required background checks.

Professionalism

For Users

Provide a safe working environment. Secure pets, provide clear instructions, and be available to grant access at the scheduled time.

Misrepresenting skills, falsifying hours, theft of property, or using stolen credit cards is strictly prohibited.
