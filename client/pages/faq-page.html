Getting Started

Everything you need to know about setting up your account and making your first booking.

How do I create an account? expand_more
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. You can register using your email address, phone number, or by connecting your Google or Apple account. Once registered, you'll need to verify your email to start booking services.
Is there a fee to join Helpers? expand_more
Signing up for Helpers as a customer is completely free. You only pay when you book a service. For service providers (Helpers), there are no upfront joining fees, but a small commission is deducted from completed jobs to cover platform maintenance and support.
How do I verify my identity? expand_more
Identity verification helps keep our community safe. Go to Account Settings > Trust & Safety to upload a government-issued ID. Verification typically takes less than 24 hours.
What areas do you service? expand_more
We are currently operating in major metropolitan areas. You can check if we service your specific neighborhood by entering your zip code on the homepage or during the booking process.
