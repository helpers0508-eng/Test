Refund & Cancellation Policy

update

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

Introduction

At Helpers, we strive to ensure fair and transparent interactions for all our users. This policy outlines the terms regarding cancellations, refunds, and rescheduling for services booked through our platform. By using Helpers, you agree to these terms to ensure a reliable experience for both customers and service providers.

schedule

Cancellation Window & Fees

Free Cancellation: You can cancel any booking for free up to 2 hours before the scheduled service time.

Time of Cancellation Fee Incurred
> 2 hours before No Fee
1-2 hours before 15% of booking value
< 1 hour before 50% of booking value
During service / No-show 100% of booking value
currency_exchange

Refund Eligibility

We are committed to quality. If you are unsatisfied with the service provided, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund under the following conditions:

person_off

Helper No-Show

If the assigned helper does not arrive within 30 minutes of the scheduled time, you are eligible for a 100% refund.

thumb_down

Quality Issues

If the service delivered was significantly different from the description or of poor quality. Validated by photo evidence.

cancel

Helper Cancellation

If a helper cancels on you less than 24 hours before the service, you receive a full refund plus a 10% credit.

verified_user

Safety Concerns

Immediate refund if the service could not be completed due to legitimate safety concerns reported at the start.

pending_actions

Processing Timelines

  • Wallet Refunds: Processed instantly after approval. Can be used immediately for other bookings.
  • Original Payment Method: Typically takes 5-7 business days depending on your bank's processing times.
  • Dispute Review: If a refund claim requires manual review, our team will respond within 48 hours.
edit_calendar

Rescheduling

Plans change! You can reschedule a booking without penalty up to 1 hour before the scheduled time. Rescheduling within the last hour will be treated as a cancellation and re-booking, incurring the 50% late cancellation fee on the original booking.

gavel

Dispute Resolution

If you believe a refund was incorrectly denied, you can escalate the issue to our Trust & Safety team. Please gather any relevant documentation, such as chat logs or photos, to support your claim.
